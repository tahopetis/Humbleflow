## Conventions

- **Next Up:** The single highest-priority item. Always one item. Start here.
- **Upcoming:** Prioritized queue. Move items up as Next Up is completed.
- **Icebox:** Ideas, nice-to-haves, and long-term items. Not prioritized.
- **On merge:** Agent marks the completed item `[x]` and reports the new top of Next Up.
